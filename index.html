WELCOME

The Nut Low is a site for people who are interested in playing Texas Holdem poker by joining our local KPC poker club in Stockholm, Sweden. This is a member site only, created to make it easier for members to meet, socialise and of course playing poker! The Straight Flush will be useful to schedule tournaments more frequentley. The website also aims to explain the basics of some of the most common poker games.
