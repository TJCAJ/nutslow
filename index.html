The Nut Club

Membership Fees

Annual membership fee of $20 gets you membership access to The Nut Club Poker Club & 1 members playing card.
(There will be a $20 fee for replacement cards)

Membership Perks

As a member of the The Low Nut Poker Club you enjoy free food & drink at every event (certain regulations apply).
Access to information about upcoming tournaments.
Plus that you will never worry about being alone again!

Membership Guidelines

All members must be 18 or older to play (at least 21 to drink alcohol), have appropriate attire, must provide guarantee of staying close to our local community by living in the neighbourhood upon entry at the time of sign up. All members are expected to behave in a polite orderly manor. No drunken or disrespectful behaviour will be tolerated.
